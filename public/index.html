Our game simulates the process of running a company and choosing what type of energy resources to use: Fossil Fuel, Wind, and Solar. This is a collaborative game where a lot of the players work together to sustain the CO2 ppm below 600. The more power a company uses, the more revenue it generates and the more CO2 emission it produces. Your goal is to stay in the normal range, which is between 350 and 450 ppm. If ppm goes over 600 or you run out of capital, the game is over! Choose your energy wisely!
Today's economy was built with the help of fossil fuels as its primary energy source and our economy depended on them for decades. However, it is one of the biggest problems that the earth is facing today. Due to the CO2 emission of fossil fuel, the level of CO2 on earth is getting higher and higher causing abnormal environmental behavior such as climate change. Climate change leads to sea level rise, desertification, and unpredictable weather events. We want to raise awareness that renewable energy generating methods can save the earth.
